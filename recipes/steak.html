Kielbasa and Veggies Breakfast

Description

Kielbasa and veggies is the best breakfast ever created by Abby-lee. The recipe includes kielbasa, bell peppers, onions, fennyl seeds, salt, pepper and virgin oil.

Ingredients

  • Kielbasa
  • Green Bell Pepper
  • Red Bell Pepper
  • White Onion
  • Fennyl Seed, Salt and Pepper

Steps

  1. Wash vegetables
  2. Slice bell peppers
  3. Chop white oninon in half
  4. Mix veggies in pan with olive oil, salt, pepper and fennyl seeds
